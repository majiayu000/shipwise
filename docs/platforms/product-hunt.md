# Product Hunt Source Guide

Product Hunt is best for productized launches with visual assets and a clear early-adopter story.

## Official Source

- Product Hunt Launch Guide: https://www.producthunt.com/launch

## Use When

- The project has a usable product surface.
- There are screenshots or a video.
- There is a landing/docs page.
- The launch goal includes early adopters, comments, community, or product credibility.

For pure CLI/devtools, Product Hunt is usually second wave unless the packaging is strong.

## Agent Checklist

- [ ] Product URL is live.
- [ ] Tagline is short and concrete.
- [ ] Thumbnail and gallery assets are ready.
- [ ] Maker comment is written.
- [ ] FAQ or support path exists.
- [ ] Launch day support owner is available.
- [ ] Outreach asks people to visit/comment, not directly upvote.

## Do Not

- Do not use Product Hunt as a substitute for a working product.
- Do not directly ask for upvotes.
- Do not launch with only a GitHub repo unless the product story and visuals are strong.
- Do not create a company account if platform rules prohibit it.
